Our Approach
The Quintessential Approach
I
Acquiring digital, profitable companies with proven results
We acquire digital, profitable businesses that are ready for their next chapter. Whether you’ve built a thriving SaaS product, a high-converting e-commerce store, or a content brand with an engaged audience, we’re here to help take it further.
II
Focus areas: SaaS, e-commerce with recurring revenue, content brands
Our sweet spot: Companies generating $250k to $2M in annual profit, with high margins and recurring revenue. We focus on businesses with a strong foundation—at least 3 years of consistent operations, healthy financials, and products people love.
III
Target deal size: $250k–$2M in annual revenue
We’re especially excited by niche vertical SaaS, subscription-based e-commerce, and media brands with organic traction. As founders ourselves, we understand the complexities of growth, transitions, and exits. That’s why we move quickly, communicate clearly, and operate with integrity.
IV
Respect for founders and fast, transparent transitions
Founders come to us when they’re ready for a change—or simply need a steady, trusted partner. Some are burnt out. Others want to pursue something new or shift focus. Often, they’ve raised capital and built something valuable, but know it may not scale to VC expectations. Sometimes, they’re running multiple ventures and need the right acquirer for one of them.

A general theory of creativity

It's impossible to explain creativity. it's like asking a bird, 'how do you fly?' you just do. - eric jerome dickey

In this essay, i attempt to provide a complete answer to the question, "what is creativity?" i begin with a minimal definition of creativity as the act of integrating information, then offer three secondary definitions. the first is a fundamental definition of creativity as a general process behind all phenomena. the second is the popular view of creativity — combinatorial creativity, or the ability to generate new variety by integrating spatially distant information. i conclude with the third, flow-state-plus definition of creativity, where creativity is the result of information integration in flow, but can reach higher levels where it feels like receiving insights from a higher power or experiencing spiritual illumination.
