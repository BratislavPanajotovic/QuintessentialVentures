Our Team

Ryan Junee

Founder & Managing Director

When it comes to starting new technology companies, few people in the world have as much experience as our founder, Ryan Junee. He has started 5 technology companies, with two currently worth $250M+ and one that was acquired by Google. He also started a venture capital fund that has invested in more than 35 early stage tech companies, and was previously a part-time partner at Y Combinator, the world’s most successful startup accelerator that produced companies including Stripe, AirBnB, Coinbase, OpenSea, Dropbox, Instacart, DoorDash, Reddit and many others.

Danilo Vicioso

Operating Partner

Danilo Vicioso is the Operating Partner at Quintessential Ventures. He was the CEO and Co-Founder of Main Street, a YC, Greylock and Khosla SaaS Platform. His background includes experience as an Entrepreneur in Residence at Prehype, a venture studio that collaborates with major corporations like Coca-Cola, Lego, and Citibank, working at LDV Capital and running a direct consumer autograph e-commerce business. Danilo also recently hired a GIS Military Analyst to find the highest quality waterfalls in the world and acquired/developed a retreat.
